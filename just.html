Tharlam-Academy Curriculum

Primary Level (Class 1-5)

At the Primary Level (Classes 1 to 5), students study Tibetan and English languages, along with Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies. They are also introduced to Buddhist Studies and basic ritual practices as part of their cultural and spiritual education.

Secondary Level (Class 6-8)

At the Secondary Level (Classes 6 to 8), students delve into advanced Tibetan grammar, English composition, and continue their studies in Math and Science. Buddhist Studies cover key teachings such as the Four Noble Truths and the Eightfold Path. Ritual training includes practices like making torma, performing puja, and chanting sutras.

Special Buddhist Practices

In addition to academic and ritual studies, students also learn and practice traditional spiritual activities such as Mandala Offering, Prostrations, and Guru Yoga. They actively participate in important Buddhist event celebrations, deepening their connection to Buddhist culture and teachings.

Meditation Practice

Students begin their day with mindfulness and meditation sessions guided by experienced monks.
